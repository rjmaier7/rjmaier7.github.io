Second life solutions

Second life battery solutions allow us to extend the battery's lifetime. First using it in a highly stressed environment and shifting to a less stressing second life solution is our way to achieve this.
‍
We support you to find best second life opportunities for your batteries.

Just let us know!

How it works

1
Battery Monitoring & Analytics

Monitor & optimize your battery fleet using our data driven battery platform. Improve your charging behavior and be aware of monetary battery value.

2
End of life management

Manage your batteries in just a few steps at end of life. We enable recommendations for second life or recycling according to battery health and demand.

3
Second life

Knowledge created by our battery platform increases safety & reliability to our modular second life solutions.
